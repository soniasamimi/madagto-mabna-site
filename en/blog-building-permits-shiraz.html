Guide to Building Permits and Completion Certificates in Shiraz

Navigating the administrative and technical requirements of Shiraz Municipality and the Engineering Organization is a major challenge for developers. Lack of knowledge regarding new density, parking, and facade regulations can lead to heavy fines or project shutdowns. At Madagto Mabna, we simplify this complex legal landscape for you.

Steps to Obtain a Building Permit in Shiraz

Whether your project is in high-end districts like Ghasrodasht or developing areas like Sadra, these steps are essential:

  • File Initiation: Submitting ownership documents and requesting the "Drawing Order" (Dastoor Naghshe).
  • Conceptual Design (Phase 1): Ensuring compliance with the Detailed Plan of Shiraz.
  • Engineering Approval: Technical review by the Fars Construction Engineering Organization.
  • Fee Payment & Issuance: Finalizing municipal fees to receive the permit.

Challenges of Obtaining a Completion Certificate (Payan-Kar)

The Completion Certificate confirms the building matches the approved plans. Violations in site coverage, changes in unit numbers, or fire safety non-compliance can delay this certificate for months. Madagto Mabna's commitment to "as-built" accuracy ensures your project secures this certificate swiftly and without penalties.
